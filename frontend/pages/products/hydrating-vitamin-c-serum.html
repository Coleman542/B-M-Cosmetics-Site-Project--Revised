How to Use

  1. Cleanse: Start with a clean face using your favorite cleanser
  2. Apply: Apply 2-3 drops of serum to your face and neck
  3. Massage: Gently massage in upward motions until fully absorbed
  4. Moisturize: Follow with your favorite moisturizer
  5. Protect: Always use sunscreen during the day when using Vitamin C products

Best Time to Use: Morning and evening for best results

Tip: Start with once daily application and gradually increase to twice daily as your skin adjusts.
